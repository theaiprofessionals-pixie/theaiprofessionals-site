Skeptia: Youth & AI

A youth-focused initiative exploring how to use AI in ways that strengthen judgment, preserve critical thinking, and build practical risk awareness.

Skeptia is based on a simple idea: young people should be able to work with AI without outsourcing their thinking to it. The goal is not fear or rejection, but thoughtful, grounded use.

  • Workshops built around real-world scenarios
  • Risk awareness around overreliance, misinformation, and weak verification
  • Practical habits that preserve agency, curiosity, and independent judgment
Core lens Symbiosis with AI, not passive dependence
Audience Youth, educators, and future workshop partners
Format Public resources, workshops, and applied frameworks
