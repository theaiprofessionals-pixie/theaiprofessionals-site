Flagship initiative

SafeAI Youth Initiative

A youth-focused initiative designed to build awareness of AI-related risks and provide practical strategies for responsible use.

Workshops: Practical AI Safety & Risk Awareness

Participants explore real-world AI challenges and build habits for safer, more responsible use.

  • Misinformation and hallucinations
  • Bias in AI systems
  • Overreliance on AI tools
  • Scenario-based exercises
  • Verification techniques
  • Safer prompting practices
  • Responsible decision-making

Our goal is to empower youth not just to use AI, but to use it critically, safely, and responsibly.
